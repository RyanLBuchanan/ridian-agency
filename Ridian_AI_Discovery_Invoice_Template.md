## NOTES TO YOURSELF (never appears on the invoice)

- **Terms:** "Due on receipt" is fine for discovery work at this price. For larger projects, consider **50% deposit up front, 50% on delivery** — protects you on bigger fixed-fee work.
- **This is a template for ALL clients**, including Sandy. For Sandy (small/solo, husband-and-wife operation), the **$750 Lite** version is likely the right entry point — lower friction, same doorway.
- **The path this sets up:** Discovery ($1,200) → findings roadmap → proposed project ($1–3k fixed) OR retainer ($300–500/mo). The discovery fee is deliberately low because it de-risks you in the client's eyes and leads to the larger paid work.
- **QuickBooks setup once:** save "AI Discovery & Readiness Assessment" and "Ongoing AI Advisory" as Products/Services, save this as a template, and future invoices are a 2-minute customer swap.
- **Sales tax:** professional/consulting services are non-taxable in many states but AL has specific rules — confirm your service is non-taxable before you set the default, so you don't over- or under-charge.
